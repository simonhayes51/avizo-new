Quiz Nights & Entertainment Services

The Quiz Master General offers a range of entertainment services designed to boost your venue's footfall and create memorable experiences for your customers.

Quiz Nights
-----------

Our most popular service - professional quiz nights that bring communities together for an evening of fun, laughter, and friendly competition.

What's Included:
• Professional quiz host with years of experience
• Quality sound system and all necessary equipment
• Fresh, engaging questions every week
• Multiple rounds covering various topics
• Picture rounds, music rounds, and specialist subjects
• Prize coordination and management
• Promotion and marketing support

Typical Format:
• 6-8 rounds of questions
• Mix of general knowledge and specialist topics
• Music and picture rounds
• Interval for answers checking
• 2-3 hours duration

Perfect For:
• Pubs looking to boost midweek trade
• Social clubs and community centers
• Regular weekly or monthly events
• Building a loyal customer base

Race Nights
-----------

Bring the excitement of the races to your venue with our professional race night packages. Perfect for fundraising events or just a fun night out.

What's Included:
• Pre-recorded professional race footage
• Full race night equipment and setup
• Bookmaker services (optional)
• Race cards and betting slips
• Expert hosting and commentary
• 8-10 races throughout the evening

How It Works:
Guests purchase 'horses' before each race, place bets, and cheer on their picks. It's interactive, exciting, and gets everyone involved!

Perfect For:
• Charity fundraising events
• Social club events
• End of season celebrations
• Stag and hen parties
