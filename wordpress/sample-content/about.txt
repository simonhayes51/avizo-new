Welcome to The Quiz Master General

For years, we've been bringing entertainment, excitement, and friendly competition to venues across North East England. Whether it's a traditional pub quiz, an exhilarating race night, or a special themed event, we deliver professional entertainment that keeps your customers coming back for more.

• Private Functions - Birthday parties, celebrations, and special occasions
